About SVVV Chat

Welcome to SVVV Chat, an anonymous chat platform exclusively for SVVV Indore college students. Our platform provides a secure and open environment for students to engage in meaningful conversations.

With SVVV Chat, you can discuss various topics such as:

  • Job opportunities and career guidance
  • Internship programs and experiences
  • Technical and non-technical interview doubts
  • General academic discussions and study tips
  • And much more!

The platform ensures anonymity, displaying whether a user is a senior, junior, or batchmate without revealing their names. This anonymity encourages open and honest communication among students.

Join the Discussion

Engage in meaningful conversations with your fellow SVVVians. Share experiences, seek advice, and connect with others anonymously. Our platform offers:

  • Global Chat: Participate in discussions that are visible to all SVVV students. Contribute your insights and learn from others.
  • Private Chat: Respond to specific messages privately. Connect with users who share similar interests or seek personalized advice.
  • Dashboard Insights: Track the number of people viewing your posts, the number of connections you've made, and other analytics to enhance your chat experience.
